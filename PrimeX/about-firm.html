Who We Are

Primex is a leading global investment banking, securities and investment management firm that provides a wide range of services worldwide. Primex services its clientele, ranging from individual investor to institution, with a full array of banking, investing, real estate, asset management and other financial and risk-management products and services. We employ balanced investment strategy, coupled with and utilize cutting-edge technologies to produce high-quality results in the investing arena. PRIMEX is a member of the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA), Securities Investors Protection Corporation (SIPC). From corporate finance services, retirement planning and wealth management, our financial experts are prepared to accommodate the needs of all investors.

PrimeX was founded in 1991 as a national full service investment brokerage firm by Cary Grant co-founder of Third Market Corp, Inc. Since that time our mission was to create an environment where intelligent investment professionals could offer investment opportunities to discerning investors. We've made substantial progress in accomplishing that goal with over two decades in business. While we've undergone many changes since we started the business, day to day operations are still headed by seasoned professionals. PrimeX is one of Wall Street's few remaining private investment banking firms.

PrimeX is a recognized and respected member of the investment community. We have earned this recognition through our performance with our clients head on, with initiative, insight and innovation. Our professionals offer a wealth of expertise accumulated from their experience in firms throughout the industry.

The key to our success lies in the unwavering commitment to provide a high level of service and attention to our valued clients. Every one of our clients is treated with the utmost care according to their needs and regardless of the size of their investment portfolio.

We have found that by harnessing the power and the resources of PrimeX, our clearing firm (Sterne Agee Inc.,) and combining the resources and expertise of PrimeX's Brokerage, Retirement, and Real Estate we are able to provide our clients with a diverse menu of investment products.

Who We Are

Primex is a leading global investment banking, securities and investment management firm that provides a wide range of services worldwide. Primex services its clientele, ranging from individual investor to institution, with a full array of banking, investing, real estate, asset management and other financial and risk-management products and services.

Our Journey

PrimeX was founded in 1991 as a national full service investment brokerage firm by Cary Grant co-founder of Third Market Corp, Inc. Since that time our mission was to create an environment where intelligent investment professionals could offer investment opportunities to discerning investors. We've made substantial progress in accomplishing that goal with over two decades in business.
